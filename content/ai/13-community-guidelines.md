# Community Guidelines

M1SSION is built on respect, fairness, and shared adventure. These guidelines help maintain a positive environment for all players.

## Core Principles

### Respect
- Treat all players with courtesy
- Respect others' strategies and approaches
- Accept wins and losses gracefully
- Celebrate community achievements
- Welcome new players warmly

### Fairness
- Play by the rules at all times
- Don't exploit bugs or glitches
- Report cheating if discovered
- Give credit where due
- Compete honorably

### Safety
- Never trespass on private property
- Respect local laws and regulations
- Be aware of surroundings while playing
- Don't play while driving
- Report unsafe locations to support

### Privacy
- Don't share others' personal information
- Respect players' anonymity preferences
- Don't stalk or harass other players
- Report privacy violations
- Protect your own information

## Acceptable Behavior

### Communication
**Do:**
- Share general strategies
- Offer encouragement
- Discuss game mechanics
- Report bugs constructively
- Give helpful feedback

**Don't:**
- Share exact solutions or answers
- Spam chat or forums
- Use offensive language
- Impersonate staff or other players
- Advertise or solicit

### Competition
**Do:**
- Compete fairly and honestly
- Learn from top players
- Share tips after mission completion
- Congratulate winners
- Support fair play

**Don't:**
- Cheat or use exploits
- Coordinate to manipulate results
- Create multiple accounts
- Buy/sell accounts or advantages
- Sabotage other players

### Content Creation
**Do:**
- Create fan art and content
- Share experiences on social media
- Stream gameplay (following guidelines)
- Write guides and tutorials
- Credit M1SSION appropriately

**Don't:**
- Use M1SSION trademark improperly
- Monetize official content without permission
- Create misleading or harmful content
- Leak unreleased information
- Violate intellectual property rights

## Prohibited Actions

### Absolutely Forbidden
- **Cheating**: GPS spoofing, hacking, exploits
- **Harassment**: Bullying, stalking, threats
- **Hate Speech**: Discriminatory or offensive content
- **Scams**: Fraudulent schemes targeting players
- **Illegal Activity**: Anything violating laws

### Consequences
- **First Violation**: Warning and educational notice
- **Second Violation**: Temporary suspension (7-30 days)
- **Third Violation**: Permanent account ban
- **Severe Violation**: Immediate permanent ban + legal action if applicable

## Reporting

### What to Report
- Cheating or unfair play
- Harassment or bullying
- Bugs or technical issues
- Unsafe locations or conditions
- Privacy violations
- Inappropriate content

### How to Report
1. **In-App**: Use Report button (on profiles, content, locations)
2. **Email**: support@m1ssion.eu with evidence
3. **Priority**: Critical safety issues
4. **Anonymous**: Available for sensitive reports

### What Happens After
- Reports reviewed within 24-48 hours
- Action taken if violation confirmed
- Reporter notified of outcome (privacy permitting)
- Serial reporters may be verified to prevent abuse

## Appeals Process

If you receive a penalty you believe is unfair:

1. **Review Decision**: Check email for details
2. **Gather Evidence**: Screenshots, logs, context
3. **Submit Appeal**: support@m1ssion.eu within 7 days
4. **Investigation**: We review with fresh eyes
5. **Outcome**: Final decision within 14 days
