# Subscription Tier Benefits

M1SSION offers multiple subscription tiers designed to enhance your treasure hunt experience. Here's a detailed comparison of what each tier provides.

## Base (Free)

**Access & Features:**
- Access to all missions (after early access periods)
- Standard clue reveals
- Basic BUZZ functionality (limited)
- Community leaderboards
- Basic notifications
- Standard support

**Limitations:**
- No early access to new missions
- Limited BUZZ uses per day
- No BUZZ Map access
- Standard clue reveal speed
- Ads may be displayed

**Best For:** Casual players exploring M1SSION

## Silver

**Everything in Base, plus:**
- **Early Access**: 2 hours before Base users
- **Enhanced BUZZ**: 50% more daily BUZZ credits
- **Clue Speed**: Faster clue unlock times
- **Ad-Free**: No advertisements
- **Badge**: Exclusive Silver badge
- **Priority Support**: Faster response times

**Price:** €9.99/month or €99/year (save 17%)

**Best For:** Regular players who want enhanced features

**Price:** €24.99/month or €249/year (save 17%)

**Best For:** Dedicated treasure hunters seeking advantages

## FAQ

**Can I upgrade mid-cycle?**
Yes! You'll be charged a prorated amount and upgraded immediately.

**What happens if I downgrade?**
You keep benefits until the end of your billing period, then switch to the lower tier.

**Are there family plans?**
Not currently, but each account needs its own subscription.

**Do subscriptions auto-renew?**
Yes, monthly and annual plans auto-renew unless cancelled.

**Can I pause my subscription?**
No, but you can cancel and resubscribe anytime without losing progress.
