# Norah AI Commands & Capabilities

Norah is your intelligent AI assistant within M1SSION. Here's what Norah can do and how to interact effectively.

## Basic Commands

### Information Queries
- "What is [feature/concept]?"
- "How does [system] work?"
- "Explain [term/mechanic]"
- "Tell me about [topic]"

### Help & Guidance
- "How do I [action]?"
- "Where can I find [feature]?"
- "What should I do next?"
- "Help with [problem]"

### Status Checks
- "Show my progress"
- "What's my current tier?"
- "How many BUZZ do I have?"
- "When is the next mission?"

## Advanced Features

### Contextual Assistance
Norah understands your current context and can provide relevant help based on:
- Your current location in the app
- Your mission progress
- Your subscription tier
- Your recent activity
- Time of day and game state

### Proactive Notifications
Norah can proactively notify you about:
- New missions available
- Clues unlocked
- Time-sensitive opportunities
- Special events
- Important updates

### Learning & Personalization
Norah learns from your interactions to:
- Better understand your preferences
- Provide more relevant suggestions
- Adapt communication style
- Anticipate your needs

## RAG-Powered Responses

Norah uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to provide accurate, grounded answers by:
1. Searching M1SSION's knowledge base
2. Finding relevant documentation
3. Constructing context-aware responses
4. Including source references

This means Norah's answers are:
- **Accurate**: Based on official documentation
- **Current**: Reflects latest information
- **Relevant**: Tailored to your question
- **Traceable**: Can cite sources

## Conversation Tips

### Be Specific
Instead of: "How does this work?"
Try: "How does BUZZ Map help me find treasures?"

### Provide Context
Instead of: "It's not working"
Try: "I can't enable push notifications on iOS after granting permission"

### Ask Follow-ups
Norah maintains conversation context, so you can:
- Ask clarifying questions
- Request more details
- Explore related topics

## Privacy & Data

### What Norah Knows
- Your public profile info (username, tier)
- Your game progress (clues found, missions completed)
- Your subscription status
- Your notification preferences

### What Norah Doesn't Know
- Your password
- Your payment details
- Your precise real-time location (unless you share it)
- Private messages or communications

### Data Usage
- Conversations may be analyzed to improve Norah
- Personal identifiers are anonymized in analytics
- You can request conversation deletion
- Opt-out available in Privacy settings

## Limitations

Norah cannot:
- Modify game rules or bypass restrictions
- Grant premium features outside your tier
- Provide hints beyond what's available in-game
- Make real-world predictions or guarantees
- Access systems outside M1SSION
