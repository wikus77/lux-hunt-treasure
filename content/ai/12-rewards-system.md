# Rewards & Prize System

M1SSION offers real-world prizes and virtual rewards for successful treasure hunters.

## Prize Categories

### Physical Prizes
- **Luxury Items**: Watches, jewelry, accessories
- **Tech Gadgets**: Latest devices, wearables
- **Experience Vouchers**: Travel, dining, events
- **Cash Prizes**: Direct monetary rewards
- **Gift Cards**: Popular retailers and services

### Virtual Rewards
- **XP Boosts**: Temporary or permanent bonuses
- **Free Credits**: BUZZ or BUZZ Map credits
- **Exclusive Badges**: Limited-edition achievement badges
- **Avatar Items**: Custom profile decorations
- **Premium Access**: Temporary tier upgrades

## How to Win

### Weekly Missions
- Complete mission fastest
- Use fewest BUZZ credits
- Most accurate final shot
- Consistent daily participation

### Monthly Competitions
- Highest total XP
- Most missions completed
- Longest streak
- Community votes

### Random Drawings
- All participants enter automatically
- Multiplier based on subscription tier:
  - Base: 1x entry
  - Silver: 1x entry
  - Gold: 1.25x entries
  - Black: 2x entries
  - Titanium: 3x entries

### Special Events
- Holiday treasure hunts
- Anniversary celebrations
- Partner collaborations
- Community challenges

## Prize Distribution

### Notification
- Winners notified via:
  - Push notification
  - Email
  - In-app message
- Response required within 48 hours

### Verification
Winners must:
- Verify identity (government ID)
- Provide shipping address (physical prizes)
- Accept terms and conditions
- Confirm prize choice (if options available)

### Delivery
- **Digital**: Delivered within 24 hours
- **Physical**: Shipped within 7-14 business days
- **Experience**: Vouchers sent via email
- **Cash**: Bank transfer or PayPal

### Taxes & Fees
- Winner responsible for applicable taxes
- Shipping included for physical prizes
- Currency conversion at market rate
- Receipt provided for tax purposes

### Level Benefits
Higher levels unlock:
- Exclusive missions

## Credits System

### BUZZ Credits
Used for proximity detection:
- Earned via XP progression (1 credit per 100 XP)
- Purchased in-app
- Granted with subscription
- Never expire

### BUZZ Map Credits
Used for area reveals:
- Earned via XP progression (1 credit per 250 XP)
- Purchased in-app
- Included with Gold+ tiers
- Never expire

## Leaderboards

### Weekly Rankings
Top performers each week:
- **1st Place**: Premium prize
- **2nd-3rd**: Runner-up prizes
- **Top 10**: XP bonuses and badges
- **Top 100**: Recognition in Hall of Fame

Reset: Every Monday at 00:00 UTC

### All-Time Rankings
- Persistent ranking across all missions
- Special recognition for top performers

- Hall of Fame induction

- **100 referrals**: Gold Recruiter badge + free Black tier month
