# User FAQ

## Getting Started

**Q: What is M1SSION?**
A: M1SSION is a location-based treasure hunt game where you follow clues, explore your city, and compete for prizes.

**Q: How do I start playing?**
A: Simply sign up for an account, enable location services, and start your first mission from the home screen.

**Q: Is M1SSION free?**
A: Yes! M1SSION offers a free Base tier. Premium tiers (Silver, Gold, Black, Titanium) provide enhanced features and early access to new missions.

## Gameplay

**Q: How do missions work?**
A: Each mission consists of clues that lead you to specific locations. Solve clues, visit locations, and complete challenges to progress.

**Q: What is BUZZ?**
A: BUZZ is a proximity-based feature that helps you know when you're near a mission location without giving away the exact spot.

**Q: What is BUZZ Map?**
A: BUZZ Map reveals the general area where a treasure is located, helping you narrow down your search within a city.

**Q: How many final shot attempts do I get?**
A: You can attempt the final location guess twice per day per mission to prevent abuse and maintain fair play.

## Notifications

**Q: How do push notifications work?**
A: M1SSION sends notifications for new clues, mission updates, and important announcements. You can customize which notifications you receive.

**Q: Why aren't I receiving notifications?**
A: Check that:
- You've granted notification permission
- Push notifications are enabled in app settings
- Your device isn't in Do Not Disturb mode
- You haven't exceeded daily notification limits

**Q: Can I control notification timing?**
A: Yes! Quiet hours (21:00 - 08:00 by default) prevent notifications during sleep hours. Premium tiers can customize these times.

## Account & Subscription

**Q: How do I upgrade my subscription?**
A: Visit the Account page and select your desired tier. Payment is processed securely through Stripe.

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription?**
A: Yes, you can cancel anytime. You'll retain premium access until the end of your billing period.

**Q: What happens to my progress if I downgrade?**
A: Your progress is preserved, but premium features become locked. You can access them again by upgrading.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support with your deletion request. We comply with GDPR and will remove all personal data.

## Technical Issues

**Q: The app isn't loading properly**
A: Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Check your internet connection
4. Try a different browser
5. Contact support if issues persist

**Q: Location services aren't working**
A: Ensure you've granted location permission in your browser/device settings. M1SSION needs precise location for gameplay.

**Q: I found a bug, how do I report it?**
A: Use the feedback form in the app or email support with:
- Description of the issue
- Steps to reproduce
- Device and browser information
- Screenshots if applicable

## Privacy & Security

**Q: What data does M1SSION collect?**
A: We collect only essential data: account info, location during gameplay, app usage analytics. See our Privacy Policy for details.

**Q: Is my location data stored?**
A: Location data is used for gameplay only and not permanently stored except for completed mission locations in your history.

**Q: How is my data protected?**
A: All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. We follow industry security best practices and comply with GDPR.
