## 1. BOARD GAME OVERVIEW

### 1.1 Product Concept

**M1SSION™: THE BOARD GAME**

A competitive deduction and strategy game where 2-6 players take on the roles of agents hunting for prizes across a modular city map. Players must manage their Pulse Energy, decode clues, navigate entity interference, and outwit their opponents.

**Game Identity:**
- Strategic but accessible
- 60-90 minute play time
- Replayable through modular design
- Faithful to digital game experience
- Standalone entertainment value

### 1.2 Market Positioning

**Target Audience:**
- Board game enthusiasts
- M1SSION™ digital game players
- Strategy game fans
- Escape room/puzzle enthusiasts

- Complexity: Between *Ticket to Ride* and *Pandemic*

## 3. GAMEPLAY MECHANICS

### 3.1 Game Objective

**Primary Goal:**
Collect the most Prize Points by discovering clue markers, claiming prizes, and surviving entity interference.

**Victory Conditions:**
- First to 21 Prize Points, OR
- Highest points when Prize deck depleted, OR
- Last agent standing (if all others eliminated)

### 3.2 Turn Structure

**Player Turn Phases:**

1. **Energy Phase**
   - Gain 2 Pulse Energy (max 5)
   - Apply entity effects if in their zone

2. **Action Phase** (3 actions maximum)
   - Move (1 action per space)
   - Buzz (search current location)
   - Claim Prize (if requirements met)
   - Trade (with adjacent players)
   - Use Equipment

3. **Entity Phase**
   - Draw and resolve Entity card
   - Move entity pawns if indicated
   - Apply zone effects

4. **Draw Phase**
   - Replenish hand to 3 cards

### 3.3 Core Mechanics

**MOVEMENT:**
- Orthogonal movement only
- Each space costs 1 action
- Fast Travel: Spend 2 Pulse Energy to move 3 spaces

**BUZZ ACTION:**
- Spend 1 Pulse Energy
- Roll Pulse Die
- Result determines signal strength
- Higher signal = closer to Prize
- Exact match = Prize revealed

**CLUE SOLVING:**
- Three clue types match three Prize types
- Collect matching clues to increase success chance
- More clues = better Prize options

**PRIZES:**
- Hidden value until claimed
- Claiming requires matching clues + location
- Some prizes have special abilities
- Point values: 1-5

### 3.4 Entity System

**MCP (Mission Control Protocol):**
- Provides bonuses in its zone
- Draws MCP cards = helpful effects
- Can protect from SHADOW

**SHADOW:**
- Creates danger zones
- SHADOW cards = negative effects
- Can steal Pulse Energy or cards
- Most aggressive entity

**ECHO:**
- Gives cryptic information
- ECHO cards = mixed effects
- Can reveal hidden information
- Most unpredictable entity

**Entity Movement:**
- Each entity has a pawn
- Moved by Entity cards
- Players in entity zones = special effects

## 4. AGENT ABILITIES

### 4.1 Agent Character Cards

Each player chooses an Agent with unique abilities:

**THE VETERAN**
- Start with 4 clue cards
- Once per game: Reveal all prizes in one zone

**THE HACKER**
- Draw 2 Entity cards, choose 1 to resolve
- Can "hack" equipment from adjacent players

**THE ATHLETE**
- Movement costs reduced by 1
- Can take 4 actions instead of 3

**THE ANALYST**
- May look at top 3 cards of any deck
- Clues count double for deduction

**THE OPERATIVE**
- Immune to first SHADOW effect each round
- Can move through other players

**THE INSIDER**
- Starts in any zone (not center)
- Once per game: Move any entity

### 4.2 Equipment Cards

Purchasable with Pulse Energy:

| Equipment | Cost | Effect |
|-----------|------|--------|
| Signal Booster | 2 | +1 to Buzz rolls |
| Shield | 3 | Block one SHADOW effect |
| Decoder | 2 | Treat any clue as wild |
| Jetpack | 4 | Ignore movement costs for 1 turn |
| Scanner | 3 | See adjacent zone prizes |
| Disruptor | 3 | Force entity to move |

## 5. CLUE SYSTEM

### 5.1 Clue Types

**Location Clues (Yellow):**
- Narrow down prize location
- Match to specific map zones
- Geographic hints

**Pattern Clues (Blue):**
- Reveal prize patterns
- Match to prize categories
- Logical deduction

**Signal Clues (Green):**
- Indicate proximity
- Match to signal strength
- Hot/cold mechanics

### 5.2 Clue Combinations

**Claiming Prizes:**
- 1 matching clue: 30% success (roll 1-2 on d6)
- 2 matching clues: 50% success (roll 1-3)
- 3 matching clues: 80% success (roll 1-5)
- 4+ matching clues: Automatic success

**Failed Claims:**
- Prize not revealed
- Clues discarded
- Pulse Energy spent
- Turn ends

### 5.3 Wild Clues

**ECHO Clues (Purple):**
- Count as any type
- Rare (5 in deck)
- Cannot be stolen
