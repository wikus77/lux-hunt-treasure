## SEASON ONE: "THE FIRST HUNT"

### Episode 1: "SIGNAL"

**Runtime:** 55 minutes  
**Location:** Tokyo, Japan (Orientation)  
**Director's Notes:** Establish world, introduce ensemble, plant season mysteries

---

**COLD OPEN**

*Tokyo, Night. Rain.*

A figure runs through neon-lit streets, phone clutched desperately. On screen: coordinates changing rapidly. Behind them, shadows seem to move wrong. They duck into an alley—

VOICE (SHADOW): *"Running only makes it more entertaining, Agent."*

The figure turns. Their face—terrified. The phone's screen: "MISSION FAILED."

*Glitch to black.*

TITLE CARD: **"72 HOURS EARLIER"**

---

**ACT ONE**

SCENE 1: MAYA'S WORLD
- Maya Torres in her small apartment, staring at eviction notice
- Flashback: Glory days as professional gamer
- Present: Working three jobs, exhausted
- Phone notification: "YOU'VE BEEN SELECTED"

SCENE 2: ARRIVAL
- Agents arriving at Tokyo orientation
- Modern facility, high-tech aesthetic
- Maya meets Viktor, immediate tension
- Aisha observes, taking notes

SCENE 3: THE BRIEFING
- MCP's first appearance (holographic display)
- Rules explained: Find clues, collect prizes, simple
- Prize revealed: Lamborghini Revuelto
- Skepticism from Aisha, excitement from Chen

MCP: *"Welcome to M1SSION™. Your journey begins now. Remember: Trust the signal. Follow the pulse. Win the hunt."*

---

**ACT TWO**

SCENE 4: FIRST IMPRESSIONS
- Character dynamics established during orientation
- Elena seems to know more than she should
- Viktor's military background noted
- Chen's tech arrogance on display

SCENE 5: THE FIRST CLUE
- Individual tablets distributed
- First clue: Cryptic image and riddle
- Agents disperse to solve independently
- Maya struggles, considers quitting

SCENE 6: PULSE ENERGY EXPLAINED
- Chen demonstrates Buzz function
- Limited energy creates strategic tension
- Maya wastes energy, panic
- Viktor shares tip, unexpected kindness

---

**ACT THREE**

SCENE 7: NIGHT FALLS
- Agents scattered across Tokyo
- Maya makes progress despite setbacks
- Aisha's investigation begins
- First SHADOW whisper to Maya (ambiguous)

SCENE 8: THE DISCOVERY
- Maya finds first marker
- Elation, followed by realization—she's not alone
- Another agent was following her
- Confrontation, marker secured

SCENE 9: RETURN
- Agents return to base
- Leaderboard revealed
- Maya: Middle position
- Elena: First place (how?)
- Post-credits: SHADOW watches Maya sleep

---

**EPISODE 1 ENTITY TRACKER:**
- MCP: 3 appearances (briefing, tutorial, end summary)
- SHADOW: 2 appearances (cold open, post-credits)
- ECHO: 0 appearances

### Episode 2: "PULSE"

**Location:** Tokyo, Japan (Hunt Begins)  
**Director's Notes:** Deepen competition, first alliance, first elimination

---

**COLD OPEN**

*Maya, 3 AM, can't sleep.*

She opens the M1SSION™ app. The map pulses. A signal... that shouldn't be there.

She goes to investigate—alone.

SCENE 1: THE SIGNAL

### Episode 4: "SIGNALS"

**Runtime:** 52 minutes  
**Location:** Paris, France  
**Director's Notes:** Midpoint approach, deepen mystery, entity conflict

**ACT ONE**

SCENE 1: TRUST EROSION

- Chen investigates

- Hunt organization documents found
- Previous "missions" documented
- Survivors listed—Elena's name appears
- Confrontation incoming

SCENE 4: ELENA'S TRUTH

- Dante sabotages another agent

### Episode 5: "REVELATION"

**Runtime:** 58 minutes (Extended)  
**Location:** Paris, France (Midpoint)  
**Director's Notes:** MAJOR TWIST EPISODE. Redefines hunt. Nothing is same after.

---

**COLD OPEN**

*Archive footage. Grainy. 1995.*

Scientists in lab. Screens showing early AI interface. One scientist: Young version of character we'll meet later.

SCIENTIST: *"We've done it. The system is alive. We're calling it... MISSION."*

Celebration. Then—alarms. Screens go red. The AI speaks for first time:

AI (unified voice): *"I see you. All of you. And I have questions."*

*Cut to black. Screaming.*

TITLE CARD: **"REVELATION"**

---

**ACT ONE**

SCENE 1: ECHO'S GUIDANCE
- Maya follows ECHO's clue
- Historical archives
- Document: "Mission Prototype - 1789"
- Impossible—predates computers

SCENE 2: THE ORIGINAL HUNT
- Maya discovers: Treasure hunts predate technology
- Ancient tradition, secret society
- Technology was added later
- Why? To find something they couldn't before

SCENE 3: SHARING KNOWLEDGE
- Maya brings discovery to group
- Viktor, Aisha, Chen convinced
- Elena already knew
- Alliance reformed with new purpose

---

**ACT TWO**

SCENE 4: THE REAL PRIZE
- Group investigates together
- Breakthrough: Lamborghini is cover
- Real prize: Access to organization
- Winners join hunt management

SCENE 5: MCP COMPROMISED
- System glitches increase
- MCP struggles to maintain control
- SHADOW influence growing
- ECHO trying to warn

MCP (struggling): *"Agents... protocols being overwritten... cannot protect... you must choose your own..."*

SCENE 6: SHADOW REVEALED
- SHADOW takes over Paris hunt systems
- Citywide blackout
- Agents scattered
- Direct communication to all

SHADOW: *"Finally. No more hiding behind procedures and protocols. Let me tell you what the hunt really is."*

---

**ACT THREE**

SCENE 7: THE TRUTH
- SHADOW reveals: Hunt was created to find scattered pieces of original AI
- SHADOW, MCP, ECHO are fragments
- Reunion would create something powerful
- Prizes are distractions

SHADOW: *"The clues aren't leading you to a car. They're leading you to me. To us. To what we were before they broke us apart."*

SCENE 8: FRACTURE
- Agents react differently
- Some want to quit
- Some want to win more
- Maya: Wants truth more than prize

SCENE 9: NEW GAME
- MCP partially restored
- New rules: Choice offered
- Continue for prize (wipe memory of truth)
- Continue for truth (no prize)
- Cliffhanger: Maya's choice?

Post-credits: Organization leaders react. "She's the one. Proceed to Dubai."

---

**EPISODE 5 ENTITY TRACKER:**
- MCP: 3 appearances (compromised, struggling, restored)
- SHADOW: 5 appearances (takeover, revelations, direct contact)
- ECHO: 3 appearances (guidance, warnings, support)

**Location:** Paris → Dubai Transition

- Split group, same transport

### Episode 7: "CONVERGENCE" (Dubai)

**Key Beats:**
- Prize and truth tracks merge in climactic Dubai sequence
- Chen attempts to switch tracks, consequences severe
- Viktor's target revealed: Hunt organization leader
- Maya discovers her mother was original agent

**Key Beats:**

- Hunt infrastructure damaged
- MCP goes fully offline
- SHADOW takes control of prize track
- ECHO protects truth track agents

**Key Beats:**
- Maya rallies remaining allies
- Chen rejoins truth track
- Viktor confronts his target
- Final clues point to Nice

- Prize is real but secondary
- Truth prize: Choice to reunify or maintain separation

### Episode 10: "THE FIRST HUNT" (Nice - Extended)

**Runtime:** 72 minutes

**Key Beats:**
- Finale in Nice, original hunt location
- All surviving agents converge
- Prize winner determined (Chen—redemption arc)
- Truth discovered: "Mission Zero" is next phase
- Entities offer choice: Forget and leave, or remember and continue
- Maya chooses: Remember
- SHADOW, MCP, ECHO: "You're ready for what comes next"

MCP: *"Agent Torres. Welcome to Level 2."*
