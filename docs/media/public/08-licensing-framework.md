## 1. LICENSING OVERVIEW

### 1.1 Licensing Philosophy

M1SSION™ licensing is designed to:
- Extend brand presence into new categories
- Generate revenue through partner expertise
- Maintain quality and brand integrity
- Create synergistic marketing opportunities
- Build long-term franchise value

**Core Principles:**
1. Quality over volume
2. Partner alignment with brand values
3. Consumer benefit prioritized
4. Mutual success model
5. Long-term relationship focus

### 1.2 Licensable Properties

**Primary IP Elements:**
- M1SSION™ brand name and logos
- Entity characters (SHADOW, MCP, ECHO)
- Game visual design elements
- GEO-PULSE ENGINE™ branding
- Character likenesses (if developed)
- Location/world design

**Secondary IP Elements:**
- In-game items and iconography
- Sound and music (with composer approval)
- Story elements and narratives
- Technology concepts

## 2. LICENSE CATEGORIES

### 2.1 Category Overview

| Category | Description | Typical Terms |
|----------|-------------|---------------|
| Apparel | Clothing and accessories | 10-15% royalty |
| Toys & Collectibles | Figures, games, collectibles | 8-12% royalty |
| Home & Living | Décor, housewares | 10-15% royalty |
| Consumer Electronics | Tech accessories | 8-12% royalty |
| Publishing | Books, comics, magazines | 6-10% royalty |
| Food & Beverage | Snacks, drinks, promotions | 5-8% royalty |
| Experiences | Events, attractions | Variable |
| Gaming | Video games, apps | Variable |
| Media | TV, film, streaming | Complex deals |

### 2.2 Tier Structure

**Tier 1: Strategic Partners**
- Major global brands
- Multi-year agreements
- Significant minimums
- Co-development opportunities
- Marketing commitments

**Tier 2: Category Leaders**
- Strong category presence
- 2-3 year agreements
- Moderate minimums
- Standard royalty rates

**Tier 3: Specialized Partners**
- Niche categories
- 1-2 year agreements
- Lower minimums
- Standard or reduced royalties

**Tier 4: Promotional Partners**
- Short-term promotions
- Limited use licenses
- Flat fees or promotional value
- Strict limitations

## 3. STANDARD TERMS

### 3.1 Financial Terms

**Royalty Rates:**
- Standard range: 8-15%
- Based on net wholesale
- Category-dependent
- Performance incentives possible

**Advance Payments:**
- Minimum guarantee required
- Recoupable against royalties
- Non-refundable
- Payment schedule negotiable

**Minimum Guarantees:**
- Annual minimums required
- Based on projected sales
- Shortfall payable
- Performance reviews

### 3.2 Term Length

**Standard Terms:**
- Initial term: 2-3 years
- Renewal options: 1-2 years
- Notice period: 6-12 months
- Performance-based renewal

**Early Termination:**
- Material breach
- Non-payment
- Quality failures
- Brand damage

### 3.3 Territory Rights

**Territory Options:**
- Worldwide
- Regional (NA, EMEA, APAC, LATAM)
- Country-specific
- Language-based

**Exclusivity:**
- Exclusive within category/territory
- Semi-exclusive (limited competitors)
- Non-exclusive
- Channel-specific

## 4. APPROVAL PROCESS

- Packaging designs
- Marketing plans

**Approval Stages:**
1. Concept approval
2. Design development approval
3. Pre-production approval

### 4.2 Marketing Approval

**Requires Approval:**
- Advertising creative
- Press releases
- Social media campaigns
- Event activations
- Promotional materials

**Timing:**
- 5 business days standard
- 3 days expedited
- Preapproved templates available

### 4.3 Approval Guidelines

**Always Required:**
- Logo usage approval
- Character depiction approval
- New design concepts
- Pricing strategies
