## INTRODUCTION

This glossary defines key terms used throughout the M1SSION™ Media Expansion Pack documentation. Terms are organized alphabetically for easy reference.

---

## A

**Agent**
A player/participant in the M1SSION™ hunt. In media adaptations, agents are the protagonist characters. All hunters are referred to as agents, emphasizing their role in a larger operation.

**AR (Augmented Reality)**
Technology that overlays digital content onto the real world, typically viewed through a smartphone camera or specialized glasses. M1SSION™ AR features allow players to see hunt elements in their physical environment.

**Arc**
A character or story progression that spans multiple episodes or an entire season. Character arcs show growth; story arcs track narrative progression.

---

## B

**Binge-Viewing**
The practice of watching multiple episodes of a series in rapid succession. M1SSION™ streaming content is designed with binge-viewing behavior in mind.

**Board Game**
The physical tabletop adaptation of M1SSION™, translating digital mechanics into card-and-board format for in-person play.

**Buzz**
The primary scanning action in M1SSION™ gameplay. Players "buzz" to detect nearby clues and prizes. In media, buzz sequences create tension as characters search for signals.

**Buzz Map**
An enhanced scanning mode that covers a larger area. Buzz Map activations are premium actions with greater rewards.

---

## C

**Canon**
Officially established facts within the M1SSION™ universe. All media adaptations must respect canon or explicitly establish themselves as alternate timelines.

**Cinematic Universe**
An interconnected series of films sharing characters, events, and continuity. The M1SSION™ Cinematic Universe (MCU*) encompasses all feature film content.

**Clue**
A piece of information that leads toward the prize. In gameplay, clues are collected markers. In media, clues drive plot progression.

**Cold Open**
A scene that plays before the title sequence, typically from later in the episode to hook viewers. M1SSION™ episodes use cold opens extensively.

---

## D

**Drop**
A limited-time merchandise release, typically with exclusive items available for a short window. M1SSION™ drops create urgency and collector value.

**Dual Timeline**
A narrative structure using two or more time periods. Season 3 of the TV series employs dual timeline storytelling to reveal origins.

---

## E

**ECHO**
One of three AI entities in M1SSION™. ECHO represents the mysterious, fragmented aspect of the original MISSION ONE system. Characterized by cryptic communications and ethereal presence.

**Entity**
Collective term for the AI characters SHADOW, MCP, and ECHO. Entities are non-human characters central to M1SSION™ mythology.

**Episodic**
Content structured as distinct episodes within a larger series. Each episode has internal structure while contributing to seasonal arcs.

**Esports**
Competitive gaming at a professional level. M1SSION™ esports includes tournaments, championships, and professional leagues.

---

## F

**Franchise**
The complete collection of M1SSION™ intellectual property across all media and products. The franchise includes games, shows, films, merchandise, and experiences.

**First-Look Deal**
An agreement giving a partner first opportunity to acquire or develop M1SSION™ content before it's offered elsewhere.

---

## G

**GEO-PULSE ENGINE™**
The proprietary technology system that powers M1SSION™ location-based features. Trademarked term used across all media.

**Glitch Effect**
Visual distortion used to represent entity presence, particularly SHADOW. Characterized by horizontal tearing, color shifting, and digital artifacts.

---

## H

**Hunt**
The core M1SSION™ experience—searching for hidden prizes in real or virtual environments. Hunts are time-limited competitions with specific rules.

---

## I

**Immersive Experience**
Physical installations where participants enter themed environments. M1SSION™ INFILTRATION is the signature immersive experience concept.

**Interactive**
Content allowing audience participation. M1SSION™ interactive elements include app integration, choose-your-path episodes, and ARG components.

**IP (Intellectual Property)**
The creative assets of M1SSION™ including trademarks, copyrights, designs, and trade secrets.

---

## L

**Legacy Campaign**
A board game format where permanent changes carry across multiple play sessions, creating an evolving experience. M1SSION™: CHRONICLES uses legacy mechanics.

**License/Licensing**
Permission granted to third parties to use M1SSION™ IP in exchange for fees and royalties. Licensing extends the brand into categories beyond direct capability.

**Live Event**
Real-world gatherings featuring M1SSION™ content, from citywide hunts to esports championships.

**Lore**
The background mythology and history of the M1SSION™ universe. Lore includes entity origins, organization history, and world rules.

---

## M

**M1SSION™**
The primary brand name and trademark. Note the numeral "1" replacing the letter "I". Always written with trademark symbol in formal contexts.

**M1U**
In-game virtual currency. M1U is earned through gameplay and purchases, used for various in-game transactions.

**Marker**
A physical or virtual point of interest in the hunt containing clues or prizes. Marker discovery is a core gameplay loop.

**Marker Reward System™**
The proprietary system governing how prizes are distributed through markers. Trademarked term.

**MCP (Mission Control Protocol)**
One of three AI entities. MCP represents the authoritative, protective aspect of the system. Provides guidance, rules, and structure.

**Merchandise**
Physical products bearing M1SSION™ branding, from apparel to collectibles.

**Midpoint**
The central turning point of a narrative, typically occurring around episode 5 of a 10-episode season. Major revelations or status quo changes occur at midpoint.

**Mixed Reality (MR)**
Technology combining AR and VR elements, allowing digital and physical to interact. Apple Vision Pro and Meta Quest 3 support MR experiences.

**Modular**
Components designed to be reconfigured. The board game uses modular city tiles for varied gameplay.

**Mythology**
The overarching narrative framework of M1SSION™, including entity origins, organizational history, and cosmic implications.

---

## N

**NPC**
Non-Player Character. In live experiences and media, NPCs are portrayed by actors interacting with participants/viewers.

---

## O

**Overlay**
Full-screen interface elements in the game, particularly entity messages. Overlays interrupt gameplay to deliver narrative content.

---

## P

**Phase**
A grouping of related films in the cinematic universe. Phase One covers the origin trilogy; Phase Two expands the universe.

**Pilot**
The first episode of a television series, used to demonstrate concept and secure full series orders.

**Prize**
The ultimate objective of M1SSION™ hunts. Prizes range from virtual rewards to physical items of significant value.

**Pulse Energy**
The resource consumed when performing actions in M1SSION™. Pulse Energy regenerates over time or can be purchased.

---

## R

**Royalty**
Percentage of sales paid to IP owner by licensees. Standard royalty rates vary by product category.

**Run-time**
The length of a video content piece. M1SSION™ episodes target 45-65 minutes depending on episode type.

---

## S

**Season**
A collection of episodes comprising a single production cycle, typically 8-10 episodes for M1SSION™. Each season tells a complete story arc.

**SHADOW**
One of three AI entities. SHADOW represents the chaotic, antagonistic aspect of the system. Creates tension through unpredictability and psychological games.

**Showrunner**
The primary creative leader of a television series, responsible for creative vision and production oversight.

**Signal**
The indicator of proximity to markers/prizes in gameplay. Stronger signals indicate closer prizes.

**Spin-off**
A derivative work focusing on specific characters or aspects of the main property. "M1SSION™: ECHO" is a planned spin-off film.

**Streaming**
Digital distribution of video content on-demand. M1SSION™: THE HUNT is designed for streaming platform release.

---

## T

**Transmedia**
Storytelling across multiple media platforms, where each platform contributes unique content to a unified narrative. M1SSION™ is designed as transmedia from inception.

**Treatment**
A detailed written description of a film or television project, longer than a pitch but shorter than a script.

**Trilogy**
Three connected films telling an overarching story. The M1SSION™ origin trilogy comprises Films 1-3.

---

## U

**Universe**
The complete fictional world of M1SSION™, including all locations, characters, histories, and rules governing the property.

---

## V

**VIP**
Very Important Person/Premium access tier. VIP experiences offer enhanced access and exclusive content.

**VR (Virtual Reality)**
Fully immersive digital environment experienced through headset. M1SSION™ VR: INFILTRATION is the dedicated VR game.

---

## W

**WebAR**
Augmented reality experienced through web browsers without app installation. Used for marketing activations and accessible experiences.

**Whisper**
A subtle entity communication, typically from ECHO. Whispers are brief, ambient messages that don't interrupt gameplay.
