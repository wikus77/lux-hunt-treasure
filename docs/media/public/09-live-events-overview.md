## 2. CITYWIDE TREASURE HUNTS

### 2.1 Concept Overview

**"M1SSION™ LIVE: THE REAL HUNT"**

Transform entire cities into playgrounds where participants use a combination of the M1SSION™ app and physical clues to hunt for real prizes hidden throughout urban environments.

**Scale Options:**
- Neighborhood (500-2,000 participants)
- District (2,000-10,000 participants)
- Citywide (10,000-100,000 participants)

### 2.2 Event Structure

**Pre-Event (2 weeks before):**
- Registration opens
- Team formation
- Clue previews released
- Safety information distributed

**Event Day:**
```
08:00 - Check-in opens
10:00 - Opening ceremony / briefing
10:30 - Hunt begins
       - Multiple waves if needed
       - Real-time app tracking
       - Entity appearances
14:00 - Midpoint revelations
       - Special clues released
       - Entity interventions
18:00 - Hunt concludes
18:30 - Finalist gathering
19:00 - Prize ceremony
20:00 - After-party (optional)
```

**Post-Event:**
- Results published
- Media shared
- Community highlights
- Next event announced

### 2.3 Prize Structure

**Grand Prize:**
- Significant value (€10,000+)
- Single winner or winning team
- Physical prize or experience

**Tier Prizes:**
- Top 10: Major prizes
- Top 50: Moderate prizes
- Top 100: Merchandise/credits
- All participants: Digital rewards

**Hidden Prizes:**
- Bonus discoveries
- Random rewards
- Lucky finds
- Mystery boxes

### 2.4 Safety & Logistics

**Safety Requirements:**
- Event marshals throughout
- Emergency contact system
- Medical support on standby
- Weather contingencies
- Crowd management

**Logistics:**
- Permit acquisitions
- City partnerships
- Transportation coordination
- Accessibility accommodations
- Environmental considerations

## 3. IMMERSIVE EXPERIENCE EVENTS

### 3.1 "M1SSION™ INFILTRATION"

**Concept:**
A 90-120 minute immersive experience where groups of 6-8 participants are "recruited" as agents and must complete a simulated mission in a purpose-built environment.

**Experience Flow:**
1. **Briefing Room** (15 min)
   - Character introduction
   - MCP holographic briefing
   - Team formation

2. **Training Zone** (15 min)
   - Equipment familiarization
   - Practice puzzles
   - Skill assessment

3. **Mission Zone** (60 min)
   - Multi-room experience
   - Puzzle sequences
   - Physical challenges
   - SHADOW interference
   - Climactic finale

4. **Debriefing** (15 min)
   - Results review
   - Photo opportunity
   - Merchandise area
   - Booking information

**Ticket Pricing:**
- Standard: €40-60 per person
- Premium (enhanced experience): €75-100
- Private booking: €400-600 per group

### 3.2 Venue Requirements

**Space:**
- 3,000-5,000 sq ft minimum
- Multiple rooms/zones
- Control room
- Waiting/briefing area
- Retail space

**Technology:**
- Projection mapping
- Interactive screens
- RFID tracking
- Sound system
- Lighting control
- Entity AI interface

**Location Options:**
- Permanent installations (major cities)
- Touring installation (modular)
- Pop-up experiences (events)

## 4. ESPORTS & COMPETITIVE

### 4.1 Tournament Structure

**"M1SSION™ CHAMPIONSHIP"**

Annual tournament series culminating in world championship event.

**Season Structure:**
1. **Online Qualifiers** (Months 1-3)
   - Open participation
   - Regional brackets
   - Top performers advance

2. **Regional Finals** (Months 4-6)
   - Live events in key cities
   - Top 32 per region
   - Regional champions crowned

3. **World Championship** (Month 7)
   - Top 16 global
   - Multi-day event
   - Major prize pool
   - Broadcast production

### 4.2 Competition Format

**Individual Competition:**
- Solo agent rankings
- Speed runs

**Team Competition:**
