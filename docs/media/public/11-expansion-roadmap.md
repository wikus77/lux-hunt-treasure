## 1. STRATEGIC VISION

### 1.1 10-Year Franchise Plan

M1SSION™ is designed for long-term growth across multiple entertainment verticals. This roadmap outlines the strategic expansion from mobile game to global entertainment franchise.

**Vision Statement:**
*By 2035, M1SSION™ will be recognized as a premier transmedia entertainment franchise, with active presence in gaming, television, film, live events, and consumer products—united by the core treasure hunt experience.*

### 1.2 Growth Pillars

**Pillar 1: Gaming**
- Mobile (core)
- Console/PC expansion
- VR/AR extensions
- Casual gaming

**Pillar 2: Screen Entertainment**
- Television series
- Feature films
- Streaming content
- Animated content

**Pillar 3: Physical Experiences**
- Live events
- Themed attractions
- Immersive experiences
- Esports

**Pillar 4: Consumer Products**
- Merchandise
- Licensed products
- Board games
- Publishing
