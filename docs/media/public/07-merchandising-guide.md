## 3. DESIGN GUIDELINES

### 3.1 Logo Usage

**Primary Logo:**
- Minimum size: 1.5 inches / 3.8cm
- Clear space: Logo height around all sides
- Backgrounds: Dark preferred, light acceptable

**Acceptable:**
- Full color on dark
- White on dark
- Black on light (limited use)
- Entity color variants

**Never:**
- Distort proportions
- Change colors arbitrarily
- Add effects (shadow, glow)
- Combine with other logos

### 3.2 Entity Design Rules

**SHADOW:**
- Colors: #FF3366 primary, #FF6B8A secondary
- Symbol: Inverted triangle with eye
- Mood: Dangerous, mysterious, powerful
- Products: Darker, edgier items

**MCP:**
- Colors: #00E5FF primary, #00BFFF secondary
- Symbol: Hexagon with rings
- Mood: Authoritative, protective, technological
- Products: Clean, tech-forward items

**ECHO:**
- Colors: #9966FF primary, #B388FF secondary
- Symbol: Fragmented circle with waves
- Mood: Ethereal, mysterious, gentle
- Products: Subtle, artistic items

### 3.3 Typography on Products

**Headlines:**
- Electrolize (primary)
- All caps, wide tracking

**Body Text:**
- Inter (primary)
- Sentence case
- Readable sizes

### 3.4 Quality Standards

**Materials:**
- Premium construction
- Durable finishes
- Comfortable fabrics
- Safe materials (toy safety compliance)

**Print Quality:**
- High-resolution graphics
- Color accuracy
- Durable application methods
- Wash-resistant (apparel)
