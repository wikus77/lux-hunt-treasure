## 1. INTRODUCTION TO PROGRESSION

The Leveling and Progression System provides players with a sense of growth, achievement, and investment in their M1SSION™ experience. This system tracks player advancement across multiple dimensions, rewarding consistent engagement while providing tangible benefits that enhance gameplay.

Progression serves both intrinsic and extrinsic motivation: players feel personal accomplishment while also receiving concrete gameplay advantages as they advance.

---

## 2. EXPERIENCE POINTS (XP)

### 2.1 XP Overview

Experience Points are the universal measure of player activity:

**Purpose**
XP quantifies player engagement and determines level progression.

**Accumulation**
XP is earned through virtually all meaningful gameplay activities.

**Persistence**
XP never decreases; progress is always forward.

**Visibility**
Current XP and progress to next level always visible to player.

### 2.2 XP Sources

Experience is earned from:

| Activity | Base XP | Notes |
|----------|---------|-------|
| Buzz Action | 10 XP | Core activity |
| Buzz Map Activation | 25 XP | Scaled by tier |
| Marker Discovery | 15 XP | Per marker |
| Marker Claim | 20 XP | Scaled by reward |
| Daily Login | 50 XP | First login each day |
| Streak Bonus | +10 XP/day | Consecutive days |
| Achievement | 50-500 XP | Varies by achievement |
| Mission Progress | 100 XP | Story milestones |
| Event Participation | Variable | Event-specific |

### 2.3 XP Modifiers

Actual XP earned may be modified by:

**Streak Multiplier**
Consecutive daily logins increase XP gain.

**Subscription Bonus**
Premium subscribers earn enhanced XP.

**Event Multiplier**
Special events may boost XP temporarily.

**First-Time Bonus**
New activity types receive one-time bonus.

## 3. LEVEL SYSTEM

### 3.1 Level Structure

Levels are organized as follows:

**Level Range**
Levels 1 through 100, with potential expansion.

**XP Curve**
Logarithmic curve ensuring accessible early levels and challenging late levels.

**Level Display**
Current level prominently shown in player profile and interface.

### 3.2 XP Requirements

Level progression requires increasing XP:

| Level Range | XP Per Level | Cumulative XP |
|-------------|--------------|---------------|
| 1-10 | 100-500 | ~2,500 |
| 11-25 | 500-1,500 | ~20,000 |
| 26-50 | 1,500-4,000 | ~100,000 |
| 51-75 | 4,000-8,000 | ~300,000 |
| 76-100 | 8,000-15,000 | ~750,000 |

### 3.3 Level Benefits

Each level provides concrete benefits:

**Pulse Energy Capacity**
+5 maximum energy per level.

**Detection Range**
Slight increase in marker detection radius.

**Reward Bonus**
Small percentage increase in reward values.

**Access Unlocks**
Certain content requires minimum level.

**Cost Reductions**
Minor discounts on certain actions.

## 4. RANK SYSTEM

### 4.1 Rank Overview

Ranks provide prestigious titles beyond numerical levels:

**Purpose**
Ranks offer recognition and exclusive benefits for dedicated players.

**Distinction**
Ranks represent broader achievement than just XP accumulation.

**Visibility**
Rank displayed alongside level in player identification.

| 6. Shadow Agent | 55 | 200 markers claimed |
| 7. Field Commander | 70 | Lead event participation |
| 8. Strategic Lead | 80 | 500 markers claimed |
| 9. Mission Director | 90 | Complete full mission |

**Specialist (Rank 3)**

## 10. BALANCE CONSIDERATIONS

### 10.1 Progression Pacing

Ensuring appropriate advancement speed:

**Early Game**
Rapid initial progress maintaining engagement.

**Mid Game**
Steady progress with meaningful milestones.

**Late Game**
Challenging but achievable long-term goals.

**End Game**
Continued engagement through prestige and events.

### 10.2 Benefit Scaling

Ensuring balanced advantages:

**Incremental Benefits**
Level advantages meaningful but not overwhelming.

**Soft Caps**
Diminishing returns at high levels.

**Catchup Mechanisms**
Lower-level players can compete.

**Fair Competition**
Skill remains important at all levels.

### 10.3 Engagement Curves

Optimizing player motivation:

**Regular Rewards**
Frequent small accomplishments.

**Significant Milestones**
Memorable major achievements.

**Long-Term Goals**
Aspirational targets for dedication.

**Social Recognition**
Visibility of accomplishments to others.
