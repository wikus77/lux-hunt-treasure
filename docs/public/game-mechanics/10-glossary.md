## INTRODUCTION

This glossary provides definitions for all terminology used within the M1SSION™ platform. Terms are organized alphabetically for easy reference. Trademarked terms are indicated with the ™ symbol.

---

## A

**Achievement**
A specific accomplishment within the game that is tracked and rewarded. Achievements provide XP, M1U, and recognition for completing designated objectives.

**Active Period**
The time window following a Buzz Map activation during which revealed markers remain interactable.

**Agent**
The official designation for M1SSION™ players within the game's narrative framework. Also the second rank tier (Rank 2).

**Antiforcing Model**
The comprehensive system designed to prevent exploitation, ensure fair play, and maintain game integrity. Includes detection, prevention, and response mechanisms.

**Area**
A defined geographic zone within the game world, typically circular, that can be activated through Buzz Map for marker discovery.

---

## B

**Base Radius**
The starting search radius for a Buzz Map activation before modifiers are applied.

**Buzz**
The primary action players perform to generate Pulse Energy signals, discover markers, and progress in the game. Buzzing represents active engagement with the game world.

**Buzz Map**
The interactive map interface through which players view the game world, track signals, and activate search areas for marker discovery.

**Buzz Map Activation**
The process of spending M1U to reveal markers within a calculated radius around the player's current position.

---

## C

**Capacity**
The maximum amount of a resource (typically Pulse Energy) that a player can store.

**Claim**
The action of collecting a reward from a discovered marker. Requires physical proximity and may have additional requirements.

**Confidence Score**
A numeric rating assigned by the Antiforcing Model indicating the system's certainty that an action is legitimate.

**Cooldown**
A mandatory waiting period between certain actions, preventing excessive repetition.

---

## D

**Daily Bonus**
Rewards granted to players for logging in each day, often increasing with consecutive login streaks.

**Detection Range**
The maximum distance at which a player can perceive a marker's Pulse Energy signal.

**Discovery**
The process of revealing a marker's existence on the map, either through proximity or Buzz Map activation.

---

## E

**ECHO**
One of three AI entities in the M1SSION™ narrative. ECHO provides cryptic hints and fragmented information, representing mystery and uncertainty.

**Elite**
The fifth rank tier (Rank 5), representing significant player advancement.

**Entity**
One of the three AI personalities (MCP, SHADOW, ECHO) that interact with players throughout the game.

**Event**
A special limited-time game occurrence featuring unique markers, challenges, and rewards.

**Experience Points (XP)**
The universal measure of player activity that determines level progression. Earned through virtually all gameplay activities.

---

## F

**Field Commander**
The seventh rank tier (Rank 7), representing advanced player status.

---

## G

**GEO-PULSE ENGINE™**
The proprietary location intelligence system that processes geographic data, verifies position authenticity, generates Pulse Energy signals, and prevents location spoofing.

**GPS (Global Positioning System)**
The satellite-based navigation system used to determine player geographic position.

---

## H

**Hollywood Gate**
A specific portal type requiring high-level achievement to access.

---

## I

**Interaction Radius**
The distance within which a player must be located to interact with a marker and claim its reward.

**Intensity**
A measure of signal strength or effect magnitude, used in various contexts including markers and entity interactions.

---

## L

**Leaderboard**
A ranking system displaying player standings based on various metrics such as points, discoveries, or achievements.

**Legendary Hunter**
The tenth and highest rank tier (Rank 10), representing maximum player achievement.

**Level**
A numeric indicator of player progression, increasing as XP is accumulated. Higher levels unlock benefits and capabilities.

**Location Spoofing**
The fraudulent practice of falsifying GPS coordinates, strictly prohibited and actively detected by the GEO-PULSE ENGINE™.

**Lore**
Background story information about the M1SSION™ universe, delivered through markers, entities, and achievements.

---

## M

**M1SSION™**
The name of the location-based mobile gaming platform. The "1" replaces the letter "I" in the stylized spelling.

**M1U (Mission Units)**
The primary in-game currency used for Buzz actions, Buzz Map activations, and other transactions.

**Map Matching**
The process of aligning GPS coordinates with known geographic features for improved accuracy.

**Marker**
A geographically-anchored digital object that players discover and interact with to receive rewards.

**Marker Reward System™**
The comprehensive framework governing marker creation, placement, discovery, interaction, and reward distribution.

**MCP (Mission Control Protocol)**
One of three AI entities in the M1SSION™ narrative. MCP serves as the official mission coordinator, providing briefings and guidance in an authoritative tone.

**Mission**
A defined competitive period with specific objectives, boundaries, and prize pools. Missions typically last 4-8 weeks.

**Mission Director**
The ninth rank tier (Rank 9), representing near-maximum player achievement.

**Mission Phase**
A distinct stage within a Mission's progression, each with its own objectives and content.

---

## N

**Narrative Engine**
The system responsible for delivering story content through entities, markers, and events.

---

## O

**Operative**
The fourth rank tier (Rank 4), representing moderate player advancement.

**Overlay**
A full-screen display used for entity communications, rewards, and important notifications.

---

## P

**Portal**
A special location-based interactive element providing unique experiences and rewards.

**Premium Marker**
An enhanced marker type offering superior rewards, typically requiring higher player level or specific conditions.

**Prestige**
A system allowing maximum-level players to reset for additional rewards and recognition.

**Prize Marker**
A marker directly connected to physical prize opportunities.

**Progression Engine**
The system tracking player advancement through experience, levels, ranks, and achievements.

**Pulse Energy**
The regenerating resource that powers player actions. Accumulates over time up to maximum capacity.

**Pulse Signal**
The virtual emission from markers that players detect, with strength increasing as they approach.

---

## R

**Radius**
The extent of a search area during Buzz Map activation, measured from the activation point.

**Rank**
A named tier of player advancement providing prestige and exclusive benefits. Ten ranks exist from Recruit to Legendary Hunter.

**Recruit**
The first rank tier (Rank 1), the starting rank for all new players.

**Regeneration Rate**
The speed at which Pulse Energy recovers over time.

**Reward**
Value granted to players for completing actions, including M1U, items, XP, and prize entries.

---

## S

**Session**
A continuous period of gameplay from login to logout or app closure.

**SHADOW**
One of three AI entities in the M1SSION™ narrative. SHADOW represents an adversarial presence, delivering warnings and threats in an ominous tone.

**Shadow Agent**
The sixth rank tier (Rank 6), representing advanced player status.

**Signal**
The Pulse Energy emission from markers that players can detect and follow.

**Signal Layer**
The visual representation of Pulse Energy signals on the Buzz Map.

**Specialist**
The third rank tier (Rank 3), representing early player advancement.

**Spoofing**
See "Location Spoofing."

**Strategic Lead**
The eighth rank tier (Rank 8), representing high player achievement.

**Streak**
Consecutive instances of an activity (such as daily logins) that may provide bonus rewards.

**Subscription**
A premium membership providing enhanced benefits for a recurring fee.

---

## T

**Threat Level**
A measure of SHADOW entity intensity toward a specific player, influencing message frequency and tone.

**Tier**
A category level within a system, such as Buzz Map activation tiers or subscription tiers.

**Trust Score**
An internal rating maintained by the Antiforcing Model reflecting player account integrity.

---

## U

**User Journey**
The complete sequence of experiences a player has from initial discovery through long-term engagement.

---

## V

**Validation**
The process of verifying data authenticity and compliance with expected parameters.

**Velocity**
The speed of player movement, calculated and monitored by the GEO-PULSE ENGINE™.

---

## W

**Whisper**
A subtle, non-intrusive narrative message appearing briefly during gameplay.

---

## X

**XP**
See "Experience Points."

---

## Z

**Zone**
A defined geographic area with specific characteristics or restrictions.
